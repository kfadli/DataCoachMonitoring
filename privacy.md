# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 02/09/2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how DataCoachMonitoring ("we", "our", or "the application") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our application to access your Garmin health and activity data.

By using this application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Data from Garmin Connect

When you authorize this application to access your Garmin Connect account, we collect the following types of data:

- **Health Data**: Including but not limited to:
  - Heart rate data
  - Stress levels
  - Body Battery metrics
  - Sleep data (duration, quality, stages)
  - SpO2 (blood oxygen saturation) data
  - Respiration data
  - Health summaries (steps, calories, distance, etc.)

- **Activity Data**: Including but not limited to:
  - Activity details (type, duration, distance, pace, etc.)
  - GPS coordinates (if available)
  - Heart rate during activities
  - Elevation data
  - Training metrics

### Technical Information

- OAuth tokens (stored securely for API access)
- Timestamps of API requests
- Error logs (for debugging purposes)

2. **To improve the service**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance functionality
3. **To ensure security**: Monitor for unauthorized access or suspicious activity
4. **To comply with legal obligations**: As required by applicable laws
